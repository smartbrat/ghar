About

Kunal Engineer is a committed Real Estate professional who delivers the best service to his clients when it comes to buying their dream home or guiding them thru their real estate investment. Driven by passion over the past two decades in Mumbai Real Estate industry, predominantly heading Sales and Marketing team of High-End residential landmark projects of Kalpataru, Raheja Universal, Shapoorji Pallonji and Indiabulls and now handling portfolios as a consultant and advisor to Ultra HNI clientele and various Developers in South Mumbai since 2018.

Closely woring as a preferred Marketing partner with K Raheja Corp, Lodha, Raheja Universal, Rustomjee, Sugee, Four Seasons, Oberoi, Piramal, Prestige to name a few. Having a knack of understanding at micro and macro level the intricacies of the market movement and Developers' selling and negotiation strategies thereby achieving the best possible value proposition for buyers and sellers. Successfully building a long lasting and fruitful relation with clients and their families in this wonderful journey of dealing in Real Estate as an emotion more then a product. Thriving toward customers' delight!
