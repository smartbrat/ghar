### Rules
- White (#fff) is PRIMARY background — not warm white
- Theme colors are DECORATIVE (shapes, accents) — never used as text on light backgrounds (insufficient contrast)
- Brand tints are ACTION-related (CTAs, badges, borders) — separate from theme colors
- Every color used on the page must come from this palette — no invented tints or shades
- WCAG AA compliance: Ink on white = 17.85:1, Muted on white = 5.41:1, all text passes 4.5:1
