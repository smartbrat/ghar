Nine shapes. One brand. Infinite possibilities.

Each shape is a variation on the fundamental "ghar" — home. Classic, modern, tall, wide, with doorways and windows. Together they form a visual vocabulary that's instantly recognizable and endlessly adaptable.

The arch door mark (center) becomes the primary brand symbol — a home with its door open, inviting you in.
