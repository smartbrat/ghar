Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know.

Ghar.tv Brand Connect is a content-driven brand partnership program that places real-estate-adjacent brands inside the home-buying journey. Brand integrations are delivered through sponsored editorial, branded video, creator campaigns, and event partnerships across Ghar.tv's Intelligence, Design, People, Brands, and GharTalks surfaces — not in banner ad zones.

Ghar.tv reaches a high-intent real estate audience across 650+ Indian cities and Dubai — home buyers actively researching property decisions, calculating EMIs, reading market reports, comparing projects, and evaluating ancillary services such as home loans, interior design, and smart home tech. The audience skews toward serious buyers in the ₹50 lakh to ₹20 Cr+ range.

Ghar.tv partners with real-estate-adjacent brands including home loan providers, interior design firms, building materials, furniture and décor brands, smart home technology, home insurance providers, legal services, paint companies, appliances and fittings, sustainability and green-building brands, home services, and financial services providers.

Brand Connect integrates brands inside editorial content, intelligence reports, video productions, and creator campaigns — not alongside them. Partnerships are native, full-funnel, and measured against property-specific metrics such as engagement depth, time-on-content, enquiry lift, and share-of-voice within the category — rather than impressions or click-throughs.

Yes. VideoWorks is Ghar.tv's in-house cinematic video studio. It produces branded content, creator-led videos, explainer series, and full brand stories, and distributes them across Ghar.tv's platform, YouTube channel, and social networks. All output is co-branded under Ghar.tv's editorial framing.

The Ghar.tv Creator Network is a curated network of real-estate-adjacent creators — architects, interior designers, property investors, finance educators, and home-service professionals — available for brand campaigns with category-fit and property-specific measurement. Unlike open influencer marketplaces, the network is vetted for relevance and performance against real estate intent.

Ghar.tv Brand Connect runs across five primary content surfaces: GharTalks (platform / podcast / long-form), Intelligence (rankings, reports, analysis, market data), Design (home design and interiors), People (community directory of brokers, developers, designers), and Brands (dedicated brand story pages). Campaigns typically run across two or more surfaces for full-funnel coverage.

Brands can partner with Ghar.tv's five proprietary event IPs — The India Property Show (flagship hybrid), India Luxury Property Show (HNI/UHNI), India Online Property Festival (virtual), India PropTech Expo (tech/B2B), and India Senior Living Show (niche) — as well as signature GharTalks industry events. Partnerships include title sponsorship, exhibition, stage formats, and co-branded content.

Every Brand Connect engagement comes with a tailored measurement framework — typically combining content engagement metrics (time, depth, completion), intent signals (tool usage, report downloads, enquiry forms), and brand-lift surveys. For full-funnel engagements, attribution against first-party data is included where privacy-permissible.
